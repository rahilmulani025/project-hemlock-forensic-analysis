# 🔍 Overview

**Project Hemlock** is a comprehensive **forensic financial analysis and acquisition due diligence project** conducted on **Rocky Mountain High Brands, Inc. (RMHB)**.

The project focuses on identifying:

* Financial distress indicators
* Toxic debt structures
* Shareholder dilution risks
* Operational inefficiencies
* Governance and litigation concerns
* Acquisition feasibility

The analysis was performed using:

* 📑 SEC Filings & Financial Statements
* 📊 Excel Financial Modeling
* 📈 Power BI Dashboarding
* 📋 Detailed Due Diligence Reporting

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

The primary objective of this project was to perform a **deep forensic investigation** into RMHB’s financial and operational condition and evaluate the company's viability from an acquisition and investment perspective.

Key focus areas included:

* Capital & Debt Structure Analysis
* Toxic Convertible Debt Examination
* Equity Dilution Tracking
* Revenue & Profitability Trends
* Operational and Supply Chain Risks
* Legal & Governance Risks
* Market & Growth Strategy Evaluation
* Liquidity & Survival Analysis

# 📊 Key Insights Derived

* Significant shareholder dilution due to convertible financing
* Persistent negative operating performance
* Heavy dependence on external financing for survival
* Operational inefficiencies leading to negative gross margins
* Elevated litigation and governance risks
* Unsustainable debt structure and liquidity concerns
